## What this script does

   1. Location: Creates a geographical point on Earth.
   2. get_solarposition: Finds exactly where the sun is in the sky (angle and direction) for your timestamps.
   3. get_clearsky: Estimates how much solar energy hits the ground assuming there are no clouds. It outputs:
   * GHI (Global Horizontal Irradiance): Total solar radiation on a flat surface.
      * DNI (Direct Normal Irradiance): Radiation hitting a surface directly facing the sun.
      * DHI (Diffuse Horizontal Irradiance): Scattered radiation from the sky.
